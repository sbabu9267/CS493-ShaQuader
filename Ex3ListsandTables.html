How can I cook spaghetti?

  1. Boil water in a large pot.
    • use at least 4 quartz of water for every pound of noodles
    • put on high heat
  2. Salt the water with at least a tablespoon — more is fine
  3. Add pasta
  4. Stir pasta
  5. Drain the pasta
